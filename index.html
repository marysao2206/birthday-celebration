Happy 20th Birthday,
Mary Sao

22 July 2006 – 22 July 2026

Today, I celebrate not only my birthday but also my journey.

Looking back, life has not always been easy. There were times when I felt lost, disappointed, and unsure of my future. I made mistakes, faced failures, and sometimes wanted to give up. But every challenge made me stronger and taught me to be patient, brave, and never stop believing in myself.

I am proud of myself for not giving up. Every late night of studying, every small step forward, every lesson I learned, and every challenge I overcame has helped me become the person I am today.

I know my journey is still just beginning. There are many dreams to chase, many lessons to learn, and many goals to achieve. I believe that every experience good or bad is helping me become a better person.

As I turn 20, I choose to welcome this new chapter with hope, gratitude, and confidence. I will keep learning, keep growing, and keep moving forward, no matter how difficult the road may be.

Happy 20th Birthday to me. ❤️ The best is yet to come.
